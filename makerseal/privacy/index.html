Privacy Policy

MakerSeal — Last updated: February 26, 2026

Overview

MakerSeal is a video watermarking app that processes all content entirely on your device. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data.

MakerSeal does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information, usage data, analytics, or tracking information. No data leaves your device.

On-Device Processing

All video processing, watermark rendering, and logo compositing happens entirely on your device using hardware-accelerated rendering. Your videos are never uploaded to any server.

Local Storage

MakerSeal stores project settings, brand presets, and thumbnails locally on your device in encrypted form. This data is never transmitted off your device and is removed when you delete the app.

Subscriptions

MakerSeal Pro subscriptions are managed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Subscription status is verified on-device using Apple's StoreKit framework.

Third-Party Services

MakerSeal does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

MakerSeal does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app does not collect information from any user.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the revised version on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@edge-accel.dev.
