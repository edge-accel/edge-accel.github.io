Add scrolling watermarks to your videos

Getting Started

MakerSeal lets you protect your videos with dynamic scrolling watermarks and logo overlays. Here's how:

  • Import a video from Photos or Files
  • Configure your watermark text, font, color, and speed
  • Add an optional logo overlay in any corner
  • Export and save to Photos or share directly

Frequently Asked Questions

What formats are supported?
MP4, MOV, and AVI files.


What does MakerSeal Pro include?
Pro removes the "MakerSeal" prefix from watermarks, unlocks brand presets, and includes future features like AI face blur.


How do I restore my subscription?
Open MakerSeal and tap "Restore Purchases" on the upgrade screen. Your subscription is tied to your Apple ID.


Can I cancel my subscription?
Yes. Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your iPhone to manage or cancel.
